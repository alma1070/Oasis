## ✨ Core Features

* **Activities (Task & Habit):** Manage daily routines and deadlines with checklist support and progress tracking.
* **Journal & Mood Check-in:** Record daily entries and assess your mood. Connect your emotional state directly to the activities you've completed.
* **Gamification (EcoWorld & Pets):** Complete tasks and write journals to earn XP, Mind Energy, and Eco Energy. Level up and nurture your personal "EcoWorld."

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

Built 100% with Kotlin, applying **Clean Architecture** and the **MVVM** pattern:

* **UI:** Jetpack Compose, Material Design 3, Navigation Compose.
* **Architecture:** MVVM, Clean Architecture (Presentation - Domain - Data).
* **Dependency Injection:** Dagger Hilt.
* **Local Database:** Room Database.
* **Asynchronous/Reactive:** Kotlin Coroutines & Flow (Unidirectional Data Flow).
